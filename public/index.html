About Us

At GoScout, we tie technology with the world of sports profiling and athletic recruitment, to make your career a whole lot easier. Recruiters, companies, and sports profilers often have jobs that demand tedious work. Putting together profiles, reports, and such can take an incredibly long time, eating up hours of the day, lowering your overall output. All of that makes it challenging to focus on the more personally rewarding aspects of their job, affecting happiness and productivity. GoScout believes in working hard but also working smart. Through intelligently designed technology and a world-class team of innovative developers, we develop automation-focused solutions built for many facets of the sports industry. Maybe, you have an idea to speed up tasks, create certain kinds of data, or want to improve a process, but don't know where to start. The GoScout team prides itself on being real-world problem-solvers, and with new quarterly products and innovations we are always on the cutting edge of what our customers need. Give us a chance, and we will give you hope and a new perspective.
